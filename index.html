About

Operator · Builder · Founder

I'm building my way into entrepreneurship by actually running things — not waiting until I graduate to start.

I took my family's B2B laboratory-supplies business off paper. It supplied chemicals and equipment to medical colleges across Hyderabad on handwritten ledgers, with no clear view of who owed what. I built one system that tracks every order, invoice, and payment — and today I run the full cycle myself, from sourcing and quotations to deliveries, GST, and every supplier and client conversation.

Alongside my BBA at IIM Bangalore, I work on a low-GI functional-food brand and a D2C ethnic-wear label, and I build websites and tools with no-code and AI. My degree is remote — so I learn business through a screen and practice it through handshakes, and I'm happiest in the gap between the two.
